(b. 1999, Villeneuve-Saint-Georges, France) live and work in Paris. I studied at the Städelschule in Frankfurt-am-Main with prof. Haegue Yang until 2025, at the Gerrit Rietveld Academie in Amsterdam until 2022, and at the Villa Arson in Nice until 2020. My work has been presented in venues such as Komplex Gallery (Seoul, 2026) Kunstistituut Melly (Rotterdam, 2025), Vietnam Art Collection (Hanoi, 2025), La Grainaterie Centre d’art (Houilles, 2025) Delfina Foundation (London 2023); Center for Contemporary Art (Karlsruhe, 2023) and Zone2Source, (Amsterdam 2021).
